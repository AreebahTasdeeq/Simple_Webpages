Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 100g pancetta
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  3. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  4. Combine the cooked spaghetti, pancetta, and egg mixture. Toss well to coat the pasta.
  5. Serve immediately with extra grated cheese and black pepper if desired.

Chicken Alfredo

A creamy pasta dish made with tender chicken, fettuccine, and Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sprinkle salt and black pepper on both sides of the chicken breasts. In a pan, melt the butter over medium-high heat and cook the chicken until no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the pan and slice it into thin strips.
  3. In the same pan, add minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer. Reduce heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  4. Add the cooked pasta and sliced chicken to the sauce. Toss everything together and heat through.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and black pepper if desired.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick and healthy stir-fry with a variety of colorful vegetables and a flavorful sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over high heat. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds.
  2. Add broccoli, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, carrot, and mushrooms to the pan. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  3. Add snow peas and continue to stir-fry for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until everything is well coated and heated through.
  5. Serve the vegetable stir-fry hot over cooked rice or noodles.
